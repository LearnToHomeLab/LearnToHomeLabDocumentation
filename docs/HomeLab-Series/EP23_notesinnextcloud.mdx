## Categorize Notes in Folders

Hover over any note and click the three dots to categorize or create folders.

<DocImage folder="images/EP23_notesinnextcloud" name="Still 2025-02-26 153633_1.7.2.png" alt="Note options" />

Click the folder icon, type the new folder name, and press <kbd>Enter</kbd> to create it.

<DocImage folder="images/EP23_notesinnextcloud" name="Still 2025-02-26 153633_1.7.3.png" alt="Create folder" />

You can then move notes into any existing or new folders by selecting them.

<DocImage folder="images/EP23_notesinnextcloud" name="Still 2025-02-26 153633_1.7.4.png" alt="Move note to folder" />
