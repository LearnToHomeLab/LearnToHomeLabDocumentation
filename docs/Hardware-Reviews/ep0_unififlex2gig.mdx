## Overview of the Ubiquiti Flex Mini 2.5G

The Ubiquiti Flex Mini 2.5G is a compact managed switch with 5x 2.5 GbE RJ45 ports, providing affordable 2.5 Gigabit Ethernet connectivity at around $49. It is designed for home networks, small offices, or as a desk switch to connect multiple 2.5G-capable devices.

### Specifications

#### Hardware

- Ports: 5 × 2.5 GbE RJ45  
- Dimensions: 117.1 × 90 × 21.2 mm (4.6 × 3.5 × 0.8 inches)  
- Weight: 206 g (7.3 oz)  
- Enclosure: Polycarbonate  
- Power: PoE input or USB-C power adapter included  
- Power Consumption: 5W (AC/DC input), 6.4W (PoE input)  
- Operating Temperature: -20 to 45 °C (-4 to 113 °F)  

#### Performance

- Total Non-blocking Throughput: 12.5 Gbps  
- Switching Capacity: 25 Gbps  
- Forwarding Rate: 18.6 Mpps  

#### Networking Features

- Layer 2 switching  
- STP / RSTP with priorities  
- Port isolation and mirroring  
- Storm control  
- Multicast / broadcast rate limiting  
- Flow control  
- Jumbo frame support  
- Egress rate limiting  
- VLAN tagging and management  

#### Management

- Managed via UniFi Network application (v8.4.62 and later)  
- Ethernet in-band management  

#### Additional Notes

- No PoE output or link aggregation support  
- No Layer 3 or 802.1X support  
- Factory reset button included  
- LED indicators for power and traffic activity  

The Flex Mini 2.5G is a notable upgrade over the original Flex Mini, with faster speeds and improved VLAN features.

## Closing Thoughts

This switch is a great choice for adding reliable 2.5GbE to your network affordably while maintaining user-friendly features.
